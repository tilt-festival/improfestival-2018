Kaspars Breidaks (LVA)

“The Strategy Behind a Character”

22 September at 10:00 Estonia pst 5a (Tallinna Rahvaülikool)

Occupation, wants and dreams are not the only things that define a character. Even though we’re improvising it’s good to have a strategy for your character’s growth and influence in the story you’re creating. The saying: “If you have a great character, the story will find you” is absolutely true, but to develop a character that does not get stuck in the plot twists can sometimes be devastating. In this workshop, we’ll create, research and strengthen the characters.

Kaspars Breidaks has studied and performed in The Second City, Chicago and represented Latvia in improv festivals in Denmark, Netherlands, Germany, Finland, Spain, France and Estonia. He hosts a daily morning show on Radio SWH in Riga, Latvia and performs every week in short and long form shows. About a year ago he created “The Joy of Life school” where he applies improv rules and techniques to help people become more open minded and enrich their communication skills.
